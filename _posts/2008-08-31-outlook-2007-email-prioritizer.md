Saiu do <a href="http://www.officelabs.com/Pages/Default.aspx" target="_blank">Office Labs</a> um add-in para o Outlook 2007 que nos adiciona um &#8220;Do not disturb button&#8221;, o <a href="http://www.officelabs.com/projects/emailprioritizer/Pages/default.aspx" target="_blank">Email Prioritizer</a>.

> Email Prioritizer is a plug-in for Microsoft Office Outlook 2007 (running on Exchange Server) that helps you manage email overload. This concept test provides a “do not disturb” button that temporarily pauses new email arrival, and prioritizes email with a 0-3 star rating system. We hope this prototype helps you focus on the emails that are most important to you.

Consultem a <a href="http://www.officelabs.com/projects/emailprioritizer/Pages/FAQ.aspx" target="_blank">FAQ</a> para mais informação sobre este add-in e façam o download <a href="http://msofficelb.vo.llnwd.net/o25/eaf1b138-dea9-4e3f-8270-1a94bad31f3d/1.0.820.0/EmailPrioritizerSetup.exe" target="_blank">aqui</a>.
